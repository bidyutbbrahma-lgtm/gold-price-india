Gold Price FAQs

What is today's gold price in India?

Today's gold price in India is influenced by international gold prices, the USD to INR exchange rate, and domestic demand. This website provides the latest updated gold price in India for both 24K and 22K gold, refreshed daily using reliable market data.

Why does the gold price change every day in India?

Gold prices in India change daily due to fluctuations in global gold markets, currency exchange movements, inflation expectations, and geopolitical factors.

Is the gold price the same across all cities in India?

Gold prices are generally similar across major Indian cities, but small differences may occur due to local taxes, transportation costs, and jeweller margins.

What is the difference between 24K and 22K gold in India?

24K gold is 99.9 percent pure and is mainly used for investment purposes such as coins and bars. 22K gold contains other metals for durability and is commonly used in jewellery.

Is gold a good investment in India?

Gold is considered a long-term store of value in India and is often used as a hedge against inflation. Learn more about gold investment options in India.
