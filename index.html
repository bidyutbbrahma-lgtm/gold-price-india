Gold Price FAQs

What is today's gold price in India?

Today's gold price in India is derived from international gold markets and converted into Indian Rupees. This page shows gold prices per ounce, per gram, and per 10 grams.

Why does gold price change daily?

Gold prices change daily due to global demand, currency movements, inflation expectations, interest rates, and geopolitical events.

Which gold price unit is most commonly used in India?

In India, gold prices are most commonly quoted per 10 grams, while international markets use prices per troy ounce.

Is gold a good investment in India?

Gold is widely considered a long-term store of value in India and is often used as a hedge against inflation and economic uncertainty.
